Standard section opener — uppercase IBM Plex eyebrow with a gold rule, Aref Ruqaa display title, optional Lora subtitle. Use `onDark` over indigo/blue surfaces and `align="center"` for hero/section centers.

```jsx
<SectionHeading
  eyebrow="Tentang Kami"
  title="Santun & Amanah sejak 2011"
  subtitle="16 tahun melayani lebih dari 10.000 jamaah haji & umrah."
/>
```
