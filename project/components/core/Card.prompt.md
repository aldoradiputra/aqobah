Soft white surface with a subtle hairline border and low cool-tinted shadow. Set `goldTop` for premium/featured content and `interactive` for clickable cards that lift on hover.

```jsx
<Card elevation="md" padding="lg" goldTop>
  <h3>Umroh Reguler 12 Hari</h3>
  <p>Berangkat setiap pekan dari Jakarta.</p>
</Card>
```

`elevation`: flat | sm | md | lg. `padding`: none | sm | md | lg.
